How it Works

Learn How Guaranteed Giving Makes It Easier to Keep Giving

Donors Select A Cause to Start Giving To

Organizations listed on Guaranteed Giving's Directory can setup one or more causes like "Direct Giving Fund" or "Sports Program Fund" for their donors to find and give to.

Schedule A Weekly or Monthly Donation

Selected causes are added to "My Giving Account" where donors can quickly create and manage their payment amount and schedule of giving for each cause.

Review, Update and Manage "My Giving"

Donors can access their "My Giving" account online at anytime to review past donations and make updates to their payment methods and active giving schedules.

Guaranteed Giving accepts donations via major credit cards and e-checks.
