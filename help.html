What Payment Methods Are Accepted?

You can setup an account for Guaranteed Giving using the following major credit cards:

  • Visa
  • AMEX
  • Mastercard
  • JCB

Can I Pay With My Bank Account?

Not yet. We are working on bringing this feature to Guaranteed Giving in the future.

Do You Store My Credit Card Information?

No. While we do require you to enter your complete billing and credit card information, your card information is not stored with Guaranteed Giving.
